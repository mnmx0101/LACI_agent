## Interpretation Notes

- IPC Manual protocols inform LACI_agent as source grounding for IPC process, convergence, evidence reliability, geography, AFI, AMN, and quality assurance logic.
- LACI_agent should not treat IPC thresholds or evidence reliability scores as model-output scoring systems unless LACI separately defines and justifies that translation.
- Model-generated outputs can support bounded workflow roles, but cannot become actual IPC outcomes or replace IPC consensus classification.
